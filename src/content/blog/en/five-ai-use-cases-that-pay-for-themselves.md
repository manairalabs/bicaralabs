When budget is tight, you don't need a hundred AI ideas — you need the two or three that pay back fastest. These five have the clearest maths.

## 1. Customer-service triage

An agent that reads incoming messages, answers the routine 60–70%, and routes the rest with context attached. The payback is direct: faster responses, fewer staff hours on repetitive questions.

## 2. Document extraction

Turning invoices, forms, and contracts into structured data automatically. Anywhere a person retypes information from a PDF into a system, there's money on the table.

## 3. Internal knowledge assistant

A search-and-answer layer over your own documents (RAG). It pays back in time-to-answer and in onboarding — new staff get productive faster.

## 4. Report generation

Draft the weekly or monthly report from the data, ready for a human to review. Saves the most senior person's time, which is the expensive kind.

## 5. Lead qualification

Score and enrich inbound leads so sales spends time on the ones worth calling. Small lift in conversion, big lift in focus.

## The pattern

Notice what these share: **high-volume, repetitive, and measurable**. That's the shape of an AI use case that pays for itself — you can count the hours saved or the conversions gained. Start there, prove the return, and let the harder, fuzzier ideas wait until you've earned the budget.
