*Representative of the systems we build.*

## The problem

A retail group was drowning in WhatsApp messages — stock, price, hours, order status — the same questions all day, answered by staff one at a time, slowly, and never after hours.

## What we built

An AI agent that lives on the WhatsApp Business API. It reads each incoming message, answers the routine majority from the product and order data, and — when it can't, or the customer asks for a person — hands off to a human **with the whole conversation attached**, so nobody repeats themselves. An agent console gives staff the live queue, response-time metrics, and a one-tap takeover.

## The outcome

Instant first replies around the clock, roughly two-thirds of enquiries resolved without a human, and staff freed to handle the conversations that actually need judgement. Deployed on the client's own infrastructure, with guardrails on what the agent can say and do.
