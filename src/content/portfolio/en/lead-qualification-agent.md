*Representative of the systems we build.*

## The problem

A B2B services company got plenty of inbound leads — and sales burned hours chasing the ones that were never going to close, while good-fit leads went cold in the queue.

## What we built

An agent that scores and enriches every inbound lead as it arrives: it pulls in context, judges fit against the profile of past wins, and surfaces the strongest leads at the top of the pipeline with a short "why." Sales sees a ranked board instead of an undifferentiated list.

## The outcome

Sales time shifted toward the leads most likely to convert, faster follow-up on the best ones, and a pipeline that reflects priority rather than arrival order. The scoring is transparent — every score comes with its reasons — so the team trusts it instead of second-guessing it.
